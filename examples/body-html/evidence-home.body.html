Evidence search

Evidence search helps you make better, faster evidence-based decisions. It includes high quality resources from over 800 organisations - like searching your trusted work favourites in one go.

We bring together evidence on health, drugs and technologies, public health, social care, and healthcare management and commissioning in one place.

Trusted sources

Sources include the British National Formulary, Clinical Knowledge Summaries, SIGN, the Cochrane Library and Royal Colleges, Social Care Online and GOV.UK. Find out more about how we source and review our content.

Professional and student audiences

Evidence search helps to answer health and social care questions from both qualified professionals and students. We promote it to students through our Student Champions Scheme. We work with academic teaching, library staff and student leads in universities to recruit Evidence Search Student Champions.
